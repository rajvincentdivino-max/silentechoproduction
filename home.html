Eerie is a Filipino horror film set in a Catholic girls school haunted by disturbing events after the death of a student. Guidance counselor Pat Consolacion investigates the mystery and uncovers the school's dark history of abuse and neglect. With help from the spirit of a former student, she reveals the truth behind the haunting and the consequences of silence and cruelty.
